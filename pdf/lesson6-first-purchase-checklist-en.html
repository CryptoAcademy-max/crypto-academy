Lesson 6: First Purchase Safety Checklist

Use this one-page checklist before your first crypto purchase so you can slow down, verify the basics, and avoid rushed mistakes.

Before You Buy

  • Confirm the exact asset name so you do not buy the wrong coin or token.
  • Check whether your local currency, deposit method, and fees are clear before continuing.
  • Turn on two-factor authentication and keep your backup codes in a safe place.

During the First Purchase

  • Start with a small amount instead of making a large first buy.
  • Review the final order screen carefully before you confirm.
  • Do not rush because of hype, countdowns, or sudden pressure.

Right After Buying

  • Check where the asset is stored and learn the difference between exchange custody and your own wallet.
  • If you plan to move funds later, learn the network, address, and test-transfer routine first.
  • Save this checklist so you can review it again before your next purchase.
Quick reminder: a calm small first step is safer than a rushed large first purchase.
