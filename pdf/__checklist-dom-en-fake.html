Crypto Academy Checklist

Quick Fake Website Check

Check the address bar before you log in.

  • Recheck the domain
  • Use bookmarked official links first
  • Do not click ad links or DM links immediately
  • Check the address bar again before logging in
The correct domain matters more than a familiar design.
Save this so you can review it when a site feels suspicious.
Check domain | Official links | Avoid ad or DM links | Check again
